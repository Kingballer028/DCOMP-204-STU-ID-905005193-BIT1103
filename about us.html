About Us

Unlocking Sierra leone's Football Future

History

Established in 1982. Football Agency Sierra Leone was born from the recognized need for a professional, ethical platform dedicated to showcasing the immense, untapped football potential within Sierra Leone. Driven by a deep passion for the sport and commitment to national talent, our founders created an agency focused on integrity, player development, and securing pathways to elite international clubs. We are dedicated to building legacies, not just careers

Mission

To discover, develop, and represent Sierra Leonean footballers locally and internationally.
